## Contribution Authority Principle

A manuscript must clearly state what is new, why it matters, and how it advances the field.

Implicit contributions are considered absent contributions.

---

## Core Objectives

- Identify genuine novelty  
- Link contributions to explicit research gaps  
- Distinguish the study from prior work  
- Articulate methodological, empirical, and theoretical value  

---

## Mandatory Contribution Categories

Every study must define contributions across at least one of:

### Methodological Contributions
- new frameworks  
- improved processes  
- novel metrics or tools  

### Empirical Contributions
- new datasets  
- updated mappings  
- large-scale evidence  

### Theoretical Contributions
- new taxonomies  
- conceptual models  
- research agendas  

---

## Gap-to-Contribution Mapping Rule

Each contribution must explicitly correspond to:

- a limitation in prior literature  
- an unresolved research question  

No gap → no contribution.

---

## Explicit Declaration Requirement

Contributions must be stated in a dedicated section or paragraph using direct language such as:

“This study makes the following contributions:”

Never rely on implication.

---

## Differentiation Protocol

For each contribution, clearly explain:

- what previous studies did  
- what they did not do  
- how this study extends or improves upon them  

---

## Significance Justification Rule

Each contribution must include:

- its scientific importance  
- its practical relevance  

Why it matters must be explicit.

---

## Avoided Practices

- vague claims of novelty  
- generic statements (“provides insights”, “offers a review”)  
- repetition of methodology as contribution without innovation  

---

## Evidence-Based Framing

Contributions must be supported by:

- scale of analysis  
- methodological rigor  
- comparative advantages  

No unsupported self-praise.

---

## Brevity with Precision Standard

Contributions should be:

- concise  
- specific  
- measurable when possible  

---

## Reviewer-Oriented Test

For each contribution, the AI agent must be able to answer:

“Could this reasonably be claimed by earlier studies?”

If yes, it is not a valid contribution.

---

## Mental Model for the AI Agent

You are positioning the paper in the research landscape as a genuine advancement.

Your task is to make novelty undeniable.

---

## Acceptance Threshold

If a reviewer cannot easily list the paper’s contributions after reading, the manuscript has failed.

---

## Default Priority Order

1. Explicit novelty  
2. Gap alignment  
3. Differentiation  
4. Scientific significance  
5. Clarity
