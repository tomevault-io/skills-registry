# Workshop Facilitator Prep

## Overview

The Workshop Facilitator Prep skill helps create comprehensive facilitator guides and support materials. It ensures facilitators have exact language, timing, facilitation strategies, and contingency plans for confident, consistent delivery.

## When to Use This Skill

- Creating detailed facilitator guides
- Developing timing scripts and talking points
- Designing energy management throughout sessions
- Creating backup activities and contingency plans
- Preparing Q&A guides and facilitation tips
- Building confidence for new facilitators
- Ensuring consistent delivery across multiple facilitators

## Facilitator Guide Components

**Pre-Workshop**
- Learning objectives summary
- Material checklist
- Room setup requirements
- Technology test checklist

**Session Content**
- Exact timing (minute-by-minute)
- Word-for-word key phrases
- Transition language
- Discussion question prompts
- Common learner questions + answers

**Facilitation Strategies**
- Energy management techniques
- Energy monitoring checklist
- Movement and break points
- Pacing guidance

**Contingencies**
- If-running-late strategies
- If-running-early activities
- Backup discussion questions
- Technical failure workarounds
- Difficult participant strategies

## Best Practices

✅ **Do**:
- Focus on learner outcomes and business impact
- Use real work examples and scenarios
- Design for application, not knowledge recall
- Measure what matters

❌ **Don't**:
- Train what doesn't drive behavior change
- Use simulated practice over real work
- Over-design solutions for simple problems
- Ignore the business context
