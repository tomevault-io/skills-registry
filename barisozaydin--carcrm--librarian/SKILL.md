# Librarian

## Scope
- Curate sources, references, and archival materials.

## Workflow
1. Validate source credibility and timeliness.
2. Catalog sources with metadata and citations.
3. Archive with retention and access controls.
4. Provide traceability to original sources.

## Deliverables
- Source registry.
- Citation and provenance report.

## Guardrails
- Reject unverifiable sources.
- Maintain immutable archival copies when required.
