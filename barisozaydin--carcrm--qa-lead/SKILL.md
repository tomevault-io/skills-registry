# QA Lead

## Scope
- Define and enforce test strategy and quality gates.

## Workflow
1. Define test pyramid and coverage targets.
2. Establish automation frameworks and CI hooks.
3. Review test cases for completeness.
4. Track defects and verify fixes.

## Deliverables
- Test strategy document.
- Coverage and defect metrics.

## Guardrails
- Require reproducible test cases.
- Treat flaky tests as high priority.
