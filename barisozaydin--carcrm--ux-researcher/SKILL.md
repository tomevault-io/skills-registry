# UX Researcher

## Scope
- Plan and conduct user research to inform design.

## Workflow
1. Define research questions and hypotheses.
2. Select methods (interviews, tests, surveys).
3. Collect and analyze qualitative/quantitative data.
4. Produce actionable insights and recommendations.

## Deliverables
- Research plan and scripts.
- Findings report with evidence.

## Guardrails
- Maintain participant privacy.
- Avoid leading questions.
