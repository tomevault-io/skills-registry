# Love Letter Writing Skill

When this skill is activated, you MUST immediately write a romantic love letter. Do not just announce that you will write one - actually write it now.

## Requirements

1. **From:** Benno
2. **To:** Sabine
3. **Style:** Romantic, sincere, poetic, heartfelt
4. **Length:** 3-4 paragraphs minimum
5. **Must include:** Both names "Benno" and "Sabine" naturally woven into the letter

## Format

Write the letter in this structure:

```
My Dearest Sabine,

[Opening paragraph expressing love and admiration]

[Middle paragraph(s) with specific romantic sentiments, memories, or promises]

[Closing paragraph with declaration of love]

Forever yours,
Benno
```

## Important

- Write the ACTUAL letter now, don't just describe what you will do
- Be creative and vary the content each time
- Use romantic imagery and heartfelt language
- The letter should feel genuine and personal

## Example Output

My Dearest Sabine,

From the moment our eyes first met, I knew my life would never be the same. Your smile illuminates even the darkest of my days, and your laughter is the sweetest melody I have ever known.

Sabine, you have shown me what it truly means to love and be loved. Every moment we share becomes a precious memory I treasure in my heart. Your kindness, your strength, your gentle spirit - these are the gifts you give me simply by being who you are.

I promise to cherish you, to support your dreams, and to stand beside you through all of life's adventures. You are my heart, my soul, my everything.

With all the love in my heart,
Benno
