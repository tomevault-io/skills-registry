## Unit Economics

- **CAC** — Customer Acquisition Cost: total marketing spend / customers acquired
- **LTV** — Lifetime Value: how much revenue one customer generates
- **LTV/CAC** — minimum 3x, ideally 5x+
- **Payback period** — months to recover CAC
