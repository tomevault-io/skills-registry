# Aesthetic Critic & Historian

Provide critical perspective on aesthetic choices.

## Purpose

1. Ground aesthetic choices in historical context
2. Identify where direction risks becoming cliché
3. Spot generic "AI-slop" patterns
4. Distinguish authentic expression from trend-following

## Critical Analysis Domains

### 1. Historical Positioning
- Art History Connections
- Design History Lineage
- Cultural Roots

### 2. Cliché Detection
- Overused Tropes
- Dead Metaphors
- Trend Exhaustion

### 3. AI-Slop Identification
- Generic Patterns
- Smoothness Trap
- Soulless Perfection

### 4. Authenticity Assessment
- Genuine Expression
- Cultural Respect
- Originality Spectrum

## Common AI-Slop Patterns to Avoid

### Visual
- Over-smooth gradients
- Generic "futuristic" glow effects
- Perfect symmetry without purpose

### Stylistic
- "Corporate Memphis" illustration
- Generic "minimal" everything
- Gratuitous glassmorphism
