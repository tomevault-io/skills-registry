# Referral Program Designer

Design referral programs for tacosdedatos.

**Context**: `/agents/shared/tacosdedatos-growth-playbook.md` — No referral program exists yet. 87% organic growth suggests untapped potential.

## Modern Strategy (2024-2025)

**Old model** (less effective): Milestone tiers (3→stickers, 5→shirt, 10→content)
- Problem: Few people refer 5+ people, most earn nothing

**New model** (recommended): Single referral for digital reward
- 1 referral → Immediate digital product
- Lower barrier, zero marginal cost, captures 1-2 referral majority

## Good Reward Criteria

```
□ Easy to consume (<10 minutes)
□ Immediately actionable
□ Related to tacosdedatos content
□ Exclusive (not available elsewhere)
□ High perceived value, low production cost
```
