# Sponsorship Playbook

## Core Philosophy

Sponsorships should create a win-win-win: value for the sponsor (ROI), value for the reader (relevant offers), and revenue for the publication. Never sacrifice reader trust for short-term sponsor revenue.

## Understanding Sponsorship Economics

### Key Metrics

#### CPM (Cost Per Mille)
Formula: `(Sponsorship Price / Subscribers) × 1,000`

| Audience Type | Typical CPM Range |
|--------------|-------------------|
| General consumer | $10-25 |
| B2B general | $25-50 |
| B2B specialized | $50-100 |
| High-value niche | $75-150+ |

#### Effective CPM (eCPM)
Based on opens: `(Price / (Subscribers × Open Rate)) × 1,000`

A 10,000 subscriber newsletter with 50% open rate has 5,000 effective impressions.

### Pricing by Subscriber Count

| Subscribers | Price Range | Notes |
|------------|-------------|-------|
| 1,000-5,000 | $50-300 | Focus on engagement over scale |
| 5,000-15,000 | $300-800 | Professional media kit needed |
| 15,000-50,000 | $800-3,000 | Multiple placement tiers |
| 50,000-100,000 | $3,000-10,000 | Premium pricing justified |
| 100,000+ | $10,000+ | Enterprise deals, custom packages |

### Placement Types

#### Primary/Header Sponsorship
- **Location**: Top of newsletter, above content
- **Format**: Image + headline + description + CTA
- **Pricing**: 100% of base rate
- **Best for**: Brand awareness, product launches

#### Mid-Roll Sponsorship
- **Location**: Between content sections
- **Format**: Native-style, blends with content
- **Pricing**: 60-80% of primary rate
- **Best for**: Products needing context

#### Classified/Footer
- **Location**: Bottom of newsletter
- **Format**: Text-only, job listings, tools
- **Pricing**: 20-40% of primary rate
- **Best for**: Multiple sponsors per issue

#### Dedicated Send
- **Format**: Entire newsletter about sponsor
- **Pricing**: 3-5x primary rate
- **Best for**: Major launches, deep partnerships
- **Caution**: Use sparingly (max 1/month)

## Creating a Media Kit

### Essential Components

#### 1. Newsletter Overview (1 page)
```markdown
## [Newsletter Name]

**Tagline**: [One-line description]

**Mission**: [Why you exist, who you serve]

**Topics Covered**:
- Topic 1
- Topic 2
- Topic 3

**Publishing Frequency**: [Daily/Weekly/etc.]

**Founded**: [Date]
```

#### 2. Audience Demographics (1-2 pages)
```markdown
## Our Audience

**Total Subscribers**: [Number]
**Average Open Rate**: [%] (industry avg: 20-25%)
**Average Click Rate**: [%] (industry avg: 2-3%)

### Who Reads Us

**By Role**:
- Data Scientists: 35%
- Data Engineers: 25%
- Analysts: 20%
- Students/Learners: 15%
- Other: 5%

**By Location**:
- Mexico: 40%
- Spain: 20%
- Argentina: 15%
- Colombia: 10%
- Other LATAM: 15%

**By Company Size**:
- Enterprise (1000+): 30%
- Mid-market (100-999): 35%
- Startup (<100): 25%
- Freelance/Student: 10%

### Audience Interests
[Data from surveys, engagement analysis]
```

#### 3. Engagement Proof (1 page)
```markdown
## Engagement Metrics

**Key Stats**:
- [X]% open rate (vs industry [Y]%)
- [X]% click rate on sponsor links
- [X] average replies per issue

**Reader Testimonials**:
> "[Quote from reader about value]" - [Name, Role]

**Growth**:
- [X]% month-over-month subscriber growth
- [X]% year-over-year growth
```

#### 4. Case Studies (1-2 pages)
```markdown
## Sponsor Success Stories

### [Company Name]
**Campaign**: [What they promoted]
**Results**:
- [X] clicks to landing page
- [X]% click-through rate
- [Conversion/signup numbers if available]

> "[Quote from sponsor about experience]" - [Sponsor Contact]
```

#### 5. Pricing & Packages (1 page)
```markdown
## Sponsorship Options

### Primary Sponsorship - $[X]
- Top placement
- [X]x[Y] image
- Headline, description, CTA button
- Typically [X-Y] clicks

### Secondary Sponsorship - $[X]
- Mid-newsletter placement
- Smaller image or text-only
- Typically [X-Y] clicks

### Classified Listing - $[X]
- Footer section
- Text only (2-3 lines)
- Best for job listings, tools

### Package Deals
- 4-week commitment: 10% off
- 8-week commitment: 15% off
- 12-week commitment: 20% off

*Custom packages available for launches and special campaigns.*
```

#### 6. Ad Specifications
```markdown
## Creative Requirements

### Images
- **Primary**: 600×300 pixels (2:1 ratio)
- **Secondary**: 300×200 pixels (3:2 ratio)
- **Format**: PNG or JPG, under 200KB
- **Style**: Clean, minimal text overlay

### Copy
- **Headline**: 50 characters max
- **Description**: 150 characters max
- **CTA**: 25 characters max

### Links
- Include UTM parameters for tracking
- One link per placement
- No redirect chains

### Deadlines
- Creative due: 3 business days before send
- Changes accepted: Up to 24 hours before send
```

## Sponsor Evaluation Criteria

### Fit Assessment Checklist

#### Audience Alignment
- [ ] Product/service relevant to our audience
- [ ] Price point appropriate for reader demographics
- [ ] Brand values compatible with publication
- [ ] No direct competitor to existing sponsors

#### Quality Standards
- [ ] Legitimate business with good reputation
- [ ] Product/service we would genuinely recommend
- [ ] No misleading claims in ad copy
- [ ] Landing page is professional and works

#### Business Viability
- [ ] Payment terms acceptable
- [ ] Clear on deliverables and timeline
- [ ] Reasonable revision expectations
- [ ] Long-term partnership potential

### Red Flags - Do Not Accept
- Get-rich-quick schemes
- Unproven health claims
- Competitor to loyal sponsors without discussion
- Excessive tracking/pixel requirements
- Pressure for no-disclosure sponsorship
- Unreasonable performance guarantees

## Sponsor Outreach

### Finding Potential Sponsors

#### 1. Look at Similar Newsletters
- Who sponsors newsletters in your space?
- Tools: Swipe file of sponsor ads you see

#### 2. Analyze Reader Tools
- What tools does your audience use?
- What products do they ask about?

#### 3. Job Postings
- Companies hiring in your niche have budget
- Tech companies especially

#### 4. Funding Announcements
- Recently funded startups need awareness
- Check Crunchbase, TechCrunch

### Outreach Template

```markdown
Subject: Sponsorship Opportunity - [Newsletter Name] ([X]k [audience type] subscribers)

Hi [Name],

I'm [Your Name], publisher of [Newsletter Name], a [frequency] newsletter
reaching [X] [audience description] in [region/language].

I noticed [Company] is [reason they're a good fit - new product, hiring,
similar sponsor in space]. Our audience of [specific description] would
be a great fit because [specific reason].

**Quick stats:**
- [X] subscribers
- [X]% open rate
- [X]% sponsor click rate

Would you be open to a quick call to explore sponsorship options?

I've attached our media kit with full details.

Best,
[Your Name]
```

### Follow-up Cadence
- Day 0: Initial outreach
- Day 3: First follow-up (if no response)
- Day 7: Second follow-up
- Day 14: Final follow-up
- Then: Add to nurture list, try again in 3 months

## Sponsor Management

### Pre-Campaign
1. Sign agreement/IO (Insertion Order)
2. Collect creative assets
3. Review for compliance with guidelines
4. Schedule placement
5. Send preview for approval

### During Campaign
1. Send confirmation when live
2. Share real-time stats if available
3. Be responsive to questions

### Post-Campaign
1. Send performance report within 48 hours
2. Include: impressions, clicks, CTR
3. Ask for feedback
4. Propose renewal if successful

## Negotiation Guidelines

### What's Negotiable
- Price (within reason - max 20% discount)
- Package deals (volume discounts)
- Payment terms (net 30 vs. net 15)
- Creative flexibility (within guidelines)
- Reporting depth

### What's Not Negotiable
- Editorial independence
- Disclosure requirements
- Exclusivity without premium
- Performance guarantees
- Creative review rights

### Handling Price Objections

**"It's too expensive"**
> "Our rates reflect the quality and engagement of our audience. Sponsors typically see [X]% CTR, which translates to [Y] cost per click - competitive with paid channels. Would a smaller placement work for testing?"

**"We need guaranteed results"**
> "We can't guarantee specific outcomes, but I can share historical sponsor performance data. Our average sponsor CTR is [X]%, and [Y]% of sponsors renew after their first campaign."

**"We have limited budget"**
> "I understand budget constraints. We have smaller placements starting at $[X], or we could explore a multi-week package with a discount. What's your target CPM or cost-per-click?"

## Long-term Relationship Building

### Monthly Sponsor Newsletter
- Share upcoming content themes
- Highlight available placements
- Share audience insights
- Celebrate sponsor wins

### Sponsor Advisory
- Annual survey on experience
- Informal check-ins
- Early access to new offerings
- Referral program

### Exclusive Benefits for Repeat Sponsors
- First right of refusal on premium dates
- Custom audience insights
- Co-marketing opportunities
- Rate lock for annual commitments
