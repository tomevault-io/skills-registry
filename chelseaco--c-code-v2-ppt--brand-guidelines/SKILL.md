# PowerPoint Brand Guidelines

## Colors

- **Navy Blue:** #050F24 (text, headers)
- **White:** #FAF8F5 (backgrounds, text on navy)
- **Gold:** #BB934D (accents only - never backgrounds or titles)

## Typography

- **Headers:** Marcellus, 36-44pt (titles), 24-28pt (sections) and Big Numbers (40-70pt)
- **Body:** DM Sans, 16-30pt
- **Minimum:** 14pt for any text

## Critical Rules

### Titles
- Always on their own line. Never in a sub column (no bold)

### Backgrounds
- **ONLY white (#FAF8F5) or navy (#050F24) for entire slides**
- **NO sub-backgrounds, boxes, or shapes within slides**
- Title slide: Navy background, white text, light font
- Content slides: White background, navy text
- Text sits DIRECTLY on background - never in boxes

### Charts
- **Use two-column layout:** Left 40% (insights), Right 60% (chart)
- **Use one-column layout** if there is LOTS of data
- **Title on own line
- **NO background boxes** around or below charts
- Chart colors: Gold, Navy
- If context needed, use two-column; otherwise full-slide chart only

### Big Numbers
- White background only
- Numbers: Gold, 40-70pt 
- Center content
- Labels: Navy, 18-24pt
- Context text: ABOVE or BESIDE numbers, never below
- NO background boxes behind text

## Slide Types

**Title:** Navy background, white text, centered vertically and horizontally. Light thin divider lines
**Content:** White background, navy text, optional gold underline
**Chart:** Two columns (insights left, chart right) on white background
**Big Number:** numbers above, Context below,  white background (center)
**Emphasis:** Navy background, white text (use sparingly)

## General
 - Avoid clutter
