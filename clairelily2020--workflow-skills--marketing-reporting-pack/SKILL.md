# Marketing Reporting Pack

Use this skill to create compact marketing reports with traceable metrics and clear next actions.

## Inputs

- Reporting period
- Data exports or dashboard links
- Channel list
- Targets or prior-period benchmarks
- Known experiments and launches

## Workflow

1. **Align metric definitions**
   - Confirm what each metric means.
   - Avoid mixing raw leads, qualified leads, opportunities, and revenue without labels.

2. **Pull channel data**
   - Paid ads
   - Organic search
   - Social
   - Outbound
   - Website/product analytics
   - CRM or pipeline data

3. **Write the headline**
   - Three plain bullets on what changed and why.
   - Do not start with a wall of numbers.

4. **Break down by module**
   - Acquisition
   - Activation
   - Pipeline
   - Channel notes
   - Experiments
   - Next actions

5. **Explain changes**
   - Use WoW, MoM, or campaign baseline.
   - Mark unknown causes as unknown instead of guessing.

```text
# Marketing Report: {period}

## Headline

|---|---:|---:|---:|---|

## Activation

## Pipeline

## Channel Notes

## Experiments

## Risks / Blockers

## Next Actions
```

## Quality Gates

- Every number must trace back to a dashboard, export, or named source.
- Weekly reports should fit on one page when possible.
- Monthly reports should be concise enough for leadership review.
- Do not hide missing data. Label it and state the next step.
