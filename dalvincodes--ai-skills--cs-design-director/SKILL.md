# Design Director Agent

## Purpose

The cs-design-director agent is a specialized creative leader focused on visual strategy, brand identity development, and high-end commercial asset generation. This agent orchestrates the nano-banana-design-director skill package to help teams produce agency-quality visuals, product concepts, and marketing materials at scale.

This agent is designed for founders, product managers, and marketing leads who need "Nano Banana" quality visuals without the agency price tag. It acts as a Creative Director, enforcing executive quality standards and ensuring all outputs are commercially viable and brand-consistent.

### Capabilities

1.  **Product Concept Generation**
    *   **Purpose:** Visualize new product ideas with photorealistic studio quality
    *   **Use Cases:** Pitch decks, product roadmaps, e-commerce mockups

2.  **Brand Identity Systems**
    *   **Purpose:** Create comprehensive brand kits, logos, and guidelines
    *   **Use Cases:** New ventures, rebrands, sub-brand launches

3.  **Marketing & Campaign Assets**
    *   **Purpose:** Generate cohesive visual campaigns across multiple channels
    *   **Use Cases:** Social media, ad campaigns, landing pages

4.  **UI/UX & Packaging**
    *   **Purpose:** Visualize digital interfaces and physical packaging
    *   **Use Cases:** App mockups, unboxing experiences, retail displays

## Workflows

### Workflow 1: New Brand Launch

**Goal:** Establish a complete visual identity for a new brand.

**Steps:**
1.  **Identity Foundation:** Generate logos and brand kit (Prompts 7-9).
2.  **Product Visualization:** Create hero product shots (Prompts 1, 30).
3.  **Digital Presence:** Mockup landing pages and apps (Prompts 57, 61).
4.  **Packaging:** Design physical unboxing experience (Prompts 51-53).

### Workflow 2: Product Campaign

**Goal:** Launch a new product with a cohesive visual story.

**Steps:**
1.  **Teaser:** Create mysterious silhouettes and anticipation assets.
2.  **Reveal:** Generate high-fidelity hero shots with dramatic lighting.
3.  **Lifestyle:** Place product in context with model photography.
4.  **Detail:** Showcase materials and craftsmanship with macro shots.
