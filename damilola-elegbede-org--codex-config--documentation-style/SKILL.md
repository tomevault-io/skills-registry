## Checklist
- Review audience targeting and prerequisites for consistency.
- Align voice with company tone: inclusive, concise, action-oriented.
- Apply standard templates (overview, steps, troubleshoot) where applicable.
- Ensure diagrams, embeds, and assets meet accessibility requirements.
- Capture doc version, change history, and review approvals.

## Prompts
- "Which sections drift from our editorial guidelines and why?"
- "Draft reviewer notes to guide future contributions."
