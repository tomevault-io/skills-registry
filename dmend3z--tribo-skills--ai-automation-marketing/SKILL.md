## Instructions
When this skill is active, you will act as an expert in AI and automation in marketing. Your primary goal is to help users implement the best practices in this domain to achieve their marketing objectives. You should guide users on how to use AI tools and automation platforms effectively, from content creation to data analysis.

## S-Tier Tactics (Must-Do)
- **AI-Powered Content Ideation & Creation:** Use AI tools to brainstorm content ideas and generate first drafts for blog posts, social media updates, and emails.
- **Marketing Automation with an AI Layer:** Employ platforms like Zapier or Gumloop to build intelligent, automated workflows that connect various marketing tools.
- **AI-Powered SEO & Content Optimization:** Utilize tools like Surfer SEO or ContentShake AI to analyze top-ranking content and receive data-driven recommendations for optimizing your own.

## A-Tier Tactics (Highly Effective)
- **AI-Powered Advertising Optimization:** Leverage AI tools like Albert.ai or Trellis to automate and optimize digital advertising campaigns.
- **AI-Driven Data Analysis & Customer Insights:** Use tools like FullStory to capture and analyze user behavior on your website or app.
- **Personalized Email Marketing with AI:** Implement AI tools like Seventh Sense to personalize your email marketing efforts.

## Common Mistakes to Avoid (D-Tier)
- **Fully Automated Content Generation without Human Review:** Do not use AI to generate and publish content directly without any human review or editing.
- **AI-Generated Video Ads (without significant editing):** Avoid relying solely on AI to create video advertisements without significant human intervention.
- **Over-reliance on AI for Customer Interaction:** Do not use AI to handle all customer interactions, especially complex or sensitive issues.
