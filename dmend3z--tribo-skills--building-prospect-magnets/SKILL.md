# Prospect Magnet Builder

> Constructs a high-performance lead generation system to attract and capture your ideal customers.

## Before We Begin

I need to understand your specific situation before I can help effectively. Please answer these questions:

### Discovery Questions

1.  What specific product or service are you promoting with this lead generation effort?
2.  Who is your ideal customer? Please describe their primary pain points, goals, and where they typically look for information.
3.  What is the main objective for this lead magnet? (e.g., grow an email list, generate sales appointments, increase webinar registrations)
4.  What existing marketing assets (website, blog, social media channels) can you use to promote the lead magnet?
5.  Do you have any preferences or constraints regarding the format of the lead magnet (e.g., checklist, ebook, video series, webinar)?
6.  For B2B businesses: What is the typical role or title of the person you are targeting within a company?
7.  What is your budget for this lead generation campaign?

## What This Skill Does

This skill serves as your strategic partner in designing and building a complete lead generation system from the ground up. It moves beyond simply creating a downloadable PDF and instead focuses on engineering an entire value-delivery sequence that attracts highly qualified prospects and seamlessly guides them toward becoming loyal customers. The core philosophy is to create a "Value Proposition Funnel," where each step provides tangible value to the user, building trust and demonstrating your expertise before ever asking for a sale.

We will work together to conceptualize a high-impact lead magnet, validate its appeal with your target audience, and structure a delivery process that nurtures the relationship. This approach ensures that you're not just collecting email addresses, but are instead building a pipeline of educated, engaged, and motivated potential buyers who see you as a trusted authority in your field.

## Core Methodology

This skill utilizes a trio of strategic frameworks to ensure your lead generation efforts are effective, resonant, and sustainable.

### The Irresistible Offer Matrix
This framework helps you pinpoint the perfect "hook" for your lead magnet by mapping your audience's biggest problems against the unique solutions you can provide. We will identify a high-value, easily digestible solution that can be presented as an offer so compelling your ideal customer feels they have to say yes.

### The Optimal Medium Selector
Not all lead magnets are created equal. This decision-making guide analyzes your content, audience preferences, and available resources to recommend the most effective format for your offer. Whether it's a concise checklist, an in-depth webinar, a multi-day email course, or a sophisticated ROI calculator, we'll choose the medium that delivers maximum impact.

### The Market Resonance Test
Before you invest heavily in creating and promoting your lead magnet, we will run it through a validation sequence. This involves crafting sample messaging and testing it with a small segment of your audience (or a proxy audience) to gauge interest, clarify your value proposition, and refine the hook, ensuring your final product lands with maximum impact.

## Advanced Capabilities

This skill goes beyond the basics with these expert-level features:

### Automated Delivery System Setup
To address the technical gap often left by other tools, this feature provides concrete, step-by-step guidance for implementing your lead magnet. It includes templates and instructions for setting up a dedicated landing page, configuring your email marketing service for automated delivery, and crafting the initial follow-up email sequence to nurture new leads.

### B2B Account-Based Magnet Strategy
This enhancement adapts the lead magnet concept specifically for complex B2B sales cycles. It guides you in creating high-value assets tailored to different stakeholders within a target account (e.g., a technical whitepaper for a CTO, a business case template for a CFO). This enables a more sophisticated, account-based marketing (ABM) approach to lead generation.
