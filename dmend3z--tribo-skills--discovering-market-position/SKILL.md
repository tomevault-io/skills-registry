# Market Position Discovery

> Pinpoints your unique market position and amplifies your brand's gravitational pull.

## Before We Begin

To help you effectively, please answer these questions:

### Discovery Questions

1.  What is the core product or service you offer, and what fundamental problem does it solve for your customers?
2.  Describe your ideal customer in detail. Go beyond demographics—what are their motivations, frustrations, and desired outcomes?
3.  What is your primary objective for refining your market position? (e.g., increase market share, launch a new category, command a premium price, fend off a new competitor)
4.  Who are your top 2-3 competitors, and what makes customers choose them over you right now?
5.  What are your non-negotiable brand values or operational constraints? What lines will you not cross, even if it means sacrificing a market opportunity?
6.  Can you share any existing customer feedback, positive or negative, that you find particularly insightful?
7.  What is your current marketing message and how is it performing?

## Core Methodology

I synthesize insights from several proven methodologies, which I've adapted into a unified workflow. Each component builds on the last to create a comprehensive and actionable strategy.

### The Insurgent's Playbook

Inspired by challenger brand dynamics, this approach focuses on identifying and leveraging asymmetric advantages. We'll analyze the market leaders' weaknesses and the underserved needs of the market to define a strategy that uses your unique strengths—like speed, focus, or a contrarian point of view—to gain a foothold and build momentum against larger, more established players.

### Zero-Competition Zone Mapping

This methodology combines the principles of category creation and blue ocean strategy. The goal is to carve out a new market space where competition is irrelevant. We'll analyze the underlying structure of your industry and adjacent markets to identify opportunities for innovation in your product, business model, or customer experience, effectively creating a new game that only you know how to play.

### Customer Gravity Engine

This is a synthesis of the Jobs-to-be-Done theory and Value Proposition design. We'll dig deep to understand the functional, social, and emotional 'jobs' your customers are trying to accomplish. Then, we'll meticulously craft a value proposition that not only solves their problem but also aligns perfectly with their deeper motivations, creating a powerful and enduring attraction to your brand.
