# Omnichannel Content Distributor

> Repurposes a single piece of content into a suite of assets tailored for every major distribution channel.

## Before We Begin

This skill needs to understand your specific situation before it can help effectively. Please answer these questions:

### Discovery Questions

1.  What is the core product, service, or idea you are promoting with this content?
2.  Describe your ideal customer or audience member in detail. What are their primary online hangouts (e.g., LinkedIn, Instagram, specific forums)?
3.  What is the primary goal for this content campaign? (e.g., generate leads, increase brand awareness, drive sales, educate users)
4.  Please provide the original piece of content (e.g., blog post, video transcript, report). What format is it in?
5.  Are there any specific formats you absolutely need, or any channels you want to prioritize or avoid?
6.  How would you describe your brand's voice and tone? (e.g., professional, witty, academic, inspirational)
7.  What specific action do you want people to take after engaging with this content?

## What This Skill Does

This skill transforms a single, foundational piece of content into a comprehensive, multi-platform marketing campaign. It goes beyond simple format conversion by intelligently atomizing your core message and then reconstructing it into various assets specifically designed for the context and audience of each distribution channel. The goal is to create a cohesive and ubiquitous presence for your message, ensuring it resonates deeply whether it's encountered as a professional article, a viral social media clip, or an insightful email.

By analyzing the source material, this engine identifies the most potent ideas, quotes, and data points. It then uses this understanding to build a suite of content that feels native to each platform. This strategic repurposing saves you hundreds of hours of manual work while amplifying the impact of your best ideas, ensuring maximum reach and engagement from a single creative effort.

## Core Methodology

This skill is built on a three-part framework designed to deconstruct, adapt, and rebuild your content for maximum impact.

### Core Message Distillation

First, the engine performs a deep analysis of your source material to extract a 'message kernel.' This isn't just a summary; it's the foundational thesis, the most compelling arguments, the key data points, and the most memorable stories. This kernel becomes the single source of truth for all generated assets, ensuring consistency across the campaign.

### Platform-Specific Adaptation

The 'message kernel' is then tailored for the unique norms and user expectations of each target platform. A LinkedIn article requires a different structure and tone than a Twitter thread, which is different from an Instagram Reel script. This methodology adapts the content's length, style, and call to action to feel organic and engaging in each specific environment.

### Asset Cascade Generation

Finally, the skill generates a 'cascade' of content assets from the adapted message. This produces a variety of formats, including but not limited to: text-based posts, image and text combinations for visual platforms, key talking points for video scripts, and concise summaries for email newsletters. The result is a complete set of ready-to-publish materials for a full-scale content distribution push.

## Advanced Capabilities

This skill goes beyond the basics with these expert-level features:

### Engagement-Driven A/B Testing

Instead of just one version per platform, this skill can generate two distinct variations of a key asset (like a LinkedIn post or email subject line). This allows you to run A/B tests to see which messaging approach resonates most effectively with your audience, providing valuable data to refine future campaigns.

### SEO & Keyword Integration

This feature analyzes your content and suggests relevant SEO keywords and hashtags for each platform. When generating text assets for blogs or articles, it will strategically weave these keywords into the content to improve search visibility and organic reach, connecting your message with a wider audience actively searching for your expertise.

## How to Use This Skill

To use this skill, provide your core piece of content (text, a URL, or a document) and answer the discovery questions. The skill will handle the analysis and generation process. You can guide the output by specifying which platforms are your priority.

### Example Prompts

-   "Here is our latest research report on the future of AI in marketing. Please transform it into a content campaign targeting marketing executives on LinkedIn, Twitter, and a corporate blog."
-   "Take this transcript from my recent podcast episode and create a series of assets to promote it. The goal is to drive newsletter sign-ups. Focus on Instagram, Facebook, and an email summary."
-   "I've written a blog post about productivity hacks for remote workers. Generate a full content suite, including two A/B test variations for the email subject line."

## Output Format

The skill will deliver a structured folder containing all the generated content assets. Each asset will be in a ready-to-use format (e.g., .txt for posts, .png for images) and organized into sub-folders by platform (e.g., /LinkedIn, /Twitter, /Blog). A master document will provide a summary of all created assets and guidance on how to deploy them.
