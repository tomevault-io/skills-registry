# Search Visibility Orchestrator

> Builds a comprehensive, data-driven strategy to elevate your content's search engine performance.

## Before We Begin

To provide the most accurate and effective optimization plan, I need to understand your specific context. Please answer these questions before we start:

### Discovery Questions

1.  What is the full URL of the primary content piece you wish to optimize?
2.  Can you describe your ideal reader or customer for this content? What are their primary challenges or interests?
3.  What is the main keyword or search query you are targeting with this page?
4.  What is the most important business objective for this content (e.g., generate qualified leads, drive direct sales, build brand awareness, earn backlinks)?
5.  Could you list 1-3 other websites or pages that you consider the top competitors or benchmarks for this topic?
6.  For advanced analysis, can you provide key performance metrics for this URL from your analytics (e.g., current rank, click-through rate, top queries)?

## What This Skill Does

This skill acts as a strategic partner in your content marketing efforts. It moves beyond simple, automated SEO checklists to provide a deep, multi-faceted analysis of your content's potential to rank and convert. By examining your content from the perspectives of search engines, competitors, and your target audience, it produces a holistic and actionable optimization strategy.

The core philosophy is that exceptional search performance is not just about keywords or technical fixes. It's about comprehensively satisfying user intent, establishing topical authority, and creating a superior user experience. This skill synthesizes data from your input, competitor analysis, and established SEO principles to create a prioritized roadmap, ensuring your efforts are focused on changes that will make the most significant impact.

## Core Methodology

Our approach is built on three foundational pillars that work together to create content that is discoverable, engaging, and authoritative.

### Pillar 1: Audience & Intent Alignment
This involves a deep dive into the "why" behind the search query. We analyze the likely intent of the user—are they looking to buy, learn, or find a specific answer? The content is then audited to ensure its structure, language, and calls-to-action are perfectly aligned with that intent, creating a seamless and satisfying user journey.

### Pillar 2: Topical Authority & Relevance
Search engines reward content that demonstrates deep expertise. This methodology focuses on ensuring your content covers the target topic comprehensively. We analyze the semantic relationships between concepts, identify gaps in your coverage compared to top performers, and ensure your content answers the key questions your audience is asking, thereby establishing your page as an authoritative resource.

### Pillar 3: Technical Structure & Readability
For content to perform, it must be both machine-readable and human-readable. This pillar covers the critical on-page elements that search engine crawlers use to understand and index your page, such as proper heading hierarchies, descriptive meta tags, and a logical internal linking structure. Simultaneously, we assess the content for clarity, scannability, and overall readability to ensure it engages and retains human visitors.

## Advanced Capabilities

This skill incorporates expert-level features to provide a decisive competitive advantage.

### Competitive Gap & Opportunity Analysis
Instead of just looking at your page in isolation, this skill analyzes the top 3-5 ranking competitors for your target keyword. It identifies the key themes, subtopics, and content formats they use, revealing critical gaps in your own content. The output is a clear list of opportunities to create a more comprehensive and valuable resource than what currently ranks.

### SERP Feature Targeting
Ranking #1 organically is no longer the only goal. This feature analyzes the search engine results page (SERP) to identify opportunities for capturing valuable real estate like "People Also Ask" boxes, Featured Snippets, and video carousels. It then provides specific, structural recommendations for formatting your content to increase its chances of being featured in these rich results.

## How to Use This Skill

To begin, simply provide the answers to the Discovery Questions. The skill will then perform its analysis and generate a detailed report.

### Example Prompts

- "Optimize my blog post about 'cold brew coffee techniques' to rank higher and attract home baristas."
- "I want to improve the lead generation from my landing page on 'small business accounting software'."
- "Analyze my article on 'sustainable travel destinations' and provide a strategy to outperform my top three competitors."

## Output Format

The skill will deliver a detailed, multi-part report in Markdown format. It will include a prioritized list of actionable recommendations, analysis of your content against the core methodologies, a summary of the competitive gap analysis, and specific guidance for targeting SERP features. Each recommendation will be accompanied by a clear explanation of its strategic importance.
