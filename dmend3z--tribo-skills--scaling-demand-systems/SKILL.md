## Core Methodology

This skill utilizes a set of integrated frameworks to build your demand generation plan. Each component is designed to provide clarity and a systematic approach to a complex process.

### The Customer Journey Continuum

Instead of a rigid, linear funnel, this framework views the customer journey as a fluid continuum. It maps out key stages from initial awareness (Top of Funnel), through consideration and evaluation (Middle of Funnel), to the point of conversion (Bottom of Funnel). For each stage, we identify the key questions your audience is asking and the content and channels best suited to answer them.

### The Channel Prioritization Framework

This framework provides a structured method for selecting the most effective marketing channels for your business. It evaluates potential channels based on factors like audience alignment, cost-effectiveness, scalability, and your team's capabilities. This data-driven approach ensures that you invest your resources in the channels most likely to deliver a positive return.

### The Experimentation Velocity System

At the heart of this skill is a commitment to continuous improvement. The Experimentation Velocity System provides a framework for rapid, iterative testing of your marketing campaigns. Using a modified ICE (Impact, Confidence, Ease) scoring model, we prioritize A/B tests and other experiments to quickly identify what works and double down on the most effective tactics.
