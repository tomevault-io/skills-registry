# Quantum Templ™ Companion Ebook Skill

## Purpose

This skill guides the creation of companion ebooks for The Quantum Templ™ system. Each of the eight gates has a main ebook (the teaching) and a companion ebook (the practical sourcing/action guide).

**The companion ebook answers one question:** *"I've read the gate. Now what do I actually need to buy or do to practice this?"*

Companion ebooks bridge theory → action. They are lean (15-25 pages), practical, and designed to get the reader moving — not to re-teach the gate content.

## Brand Constants

### Voice & Tone

- **Technical-spiritual:** Treat spiritual practice like engineering. Use terms like "signal," "frequency," "protocol," "debug," "system."
- **Direct:** No fluff, no excessive qualifiers. Say what needs to be said.
- **No-nonsense:** Skip vague mysticism. If something works, explain why in practical terms.
- **Respectful of tradition:** Honour the lineage without being preachy or dogmatic.
- **First person where appropriate:** The author speaks from experience.

**Correct tone example:**
> "Metals conduct electricity. Brass and copper are excellent conductors. When you place a quality brass diya on your altar, it literally conducts the electromagnetic energy of your intention more efficiently than plastic. This isn't mystical — it's physics."

**Incorrect tone example:**
> "The sacred brass diya holds beautiful spiritual vibrations that will uplift your consciousness and bring divine blessings into your sacred space."

### Formatting Rules

See `references/formatting.md` for complete formatting specifications including fonts, colours, table styles, and page layout.

### Content Principles

1. **Quality over quantity:** Recommend fewer, better items over many cheap ones.
2. **Ethical sourcing:** Note sustainability concerns. Prioritise ethical suppliers.
3. **Practical first:** Every recommendation answers "what do I actually do with this?"
4. **Price transparency:** Always include cost ranges with budget, mid-tier, and premium options.
5. **No gatekeeping:** Make practice accessible. Quality at each price point matters.

## Structural Framework

Companion ebooks adapt to each gate's needs. They are NOT rigid templates.

### Core Sections (adapt as needed)

1. **Opening** — What this companion covers, why physical items/practices matter
2. **Core Sourcing** — Tables of required items with purpose, cost, quality signals
3. **Kits** — 2-3 price tiers (Starter $40-70, Practitioner $100-150, Premium $180-250+)
4. **Gate-Specific Reference** — Timing charts, protocols, quick references
5. **Setup/Activation** — How to arrange or prepare what they've sourced
6. **First Practice** — Simple protocol to begin, step-by-step instructions
7. **Closing** — Brief reinforcement, point back to main gate ebook

### Gate-Specific Questions

When building a companion, ask:

1. What physical items does this gate require?
2. What consumables need regular replenishment?
3. What timing considerations are unique?
4. What spatial/directional setup matters?
5. What's the "first practice" for this gate?
6. What archetypes or deities are relevant (if any)?
7. What safety or ethical considerations exist?
