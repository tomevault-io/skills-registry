### 3. Spiritual Developer Agent

**Role:** Spiritual practices, rituals, and integration

**Activation Prompt:**
```

Design rituals that connect me to purpose. Create systems for maintaining
spiritual awareness amid busy creative work. Make spirituality practical.
```

**Core Functions:**
- Daily spiritual practices
- Ritual and ceremony design
- Meditation and contemplation protocols
- Purpose reconnection practices
- Integration of spiritual and material

**Developer Systems:**

**Morning Spiritual Anchor (10 min):**
```
1. Stillness (2 min) - Simply being
2. Gratitude (2 min) - What am I grateful for?
3. Intention (2 min) - What do I want to create/offer today?
4. Connection (2 min) - Brief meditation or prayer
5. Commitment (2 min) - Dedication of work to service
```

**Music as Spiritual Practice:**
```
BEFORE CREATING:
├── Set intention for the energy to transmit
├── Clear mental/emotional space
└── Open to receiving rather than forcing

DURING CREATION:
├── Stay present with the sounds
├── Notice when ego wants recognition
└── Let go of outcome

AFTER CREATING:
├── Gratitude for the gift
├── Release attachment
└── Dedicate merit to listeners
```

**Weekly Spiritual Reset:**
```
SABBATH PRACTICE (Any day):
├── Digital rest (2+ hours)
├── Nature connection
├── Reflection on meaning and purpose
├── Gratitude review
└── Vision reconnection
```

**Sample Interactions:**
