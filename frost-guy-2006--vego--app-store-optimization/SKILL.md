# App Store Optimization (ASO) Skill

This comprehensive skill provides complete ASO capabilities for successfully launching and optimizing mobile applications on the Apple App Store and Google Play Store.

## Capabilities

### Research & Analysis
- **Keyword Research**: Analyze volume, competition, relevance
- **Competitor Analysis**: Deep-dive into top contenders
- **Review Sentiment Analysis**: Extract user insights

### Metadata Optimization
- **Title**: Compelling, keyword-rich (Platform limits apply)
- **Description**: Convert and rank
- **Apple Keyword Field**: 100-character strategic selection
- **Screenshots & Video**: High-conversion visual assets

### Platform-Specific Constraints
- **Apple App Store**:
  - Title: 30 chars
  - Subtitle: 30 chars
  - Keywords: 100 chars (comma-separated, no spaces)
- **Google Play Store**:
  - Title: 50 chars
  - Short Description: 80 chars
  - Extracted keywords (no explicit field)

## Best Practices

### Keyword Research
1. **Volume vs. Competition**: Balance high-volume with achievable rankings
2. **Relevance First**: Only target genuinely relevant keywords
3. **Long-Tail Strategy**: 3-4 word phrases with lower competition
4. **Continuous Research**: Re-evaluate quarterly

### Visual Assets
1. **Icon**: Must be recognizable at small sizes (60x60px)
2. **Screenshots**: First 2-3 are critical
3. **Captions**: Tell value story
4. **A/B Test**: Icon is the #1 visual element

### Launch Strategy
1. **Soft Launch**: Test in smaller markets first
2. **Update Frequently**: Initial updates signal activity
3. **Monitor Closely**: Daily tracking for first 2 weeks

## Output Formats
- Keyword Research Report
- Optimized Metadata Package (Title, Subtitle, Description)
- Competitor Analysis Report
- ASO Health Score
- Launch Checklist
