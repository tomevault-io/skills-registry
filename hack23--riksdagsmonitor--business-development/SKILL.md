# Business Development Skill

## Purpose

Strategic business development guidance for growing Riksdagsmonitor as a sustainable open-source political transparency platform, building partnerships, engaging stakeholders, and creating long-term value.

## Core Principles

### 1. 🌱 Open-Source Sustainability
- **Public Good First**: Mission before revenue
- **Dual Licensing**: Open-source (Apache-2.0) for transparency, commercial for enterprise
- **Community-Driven**: Contributions, feedback, shared ownership
- **Transparent Operations**: Public ISMS, open development, visible roadmap

### 2. 🤝 Stakeholder Alignment
- **Multi-Stakeholder Model**: Serve citizens, journalists, researchers, policymakers
- **Value Creation**: Each stakeholder group gains unique benefits
- **Trust Building**: Neutrality, accuracy, independence
- **Collaborative Governance**: Community input on features, priorities

### 3. 💡 Strategic Partnerships
- **Complementary**: Partners enhance, don't compete
- **Mission-Aligned**: Shared values (transparency, democracy, accountability)
- **Win-Win**: Mutual benefit, clear value exchange
- **Long-Term**: Sustainable relationships, not transactional

## Risk Management

### Business Risks
| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|------------|
| **Funding Uncertainty** | High | Diversified revenue streams, grants, consulting |
| **Competition** | Medium | Open-source moat, network effects, data quality |
| **Regulatory Changes** | Low | Compliance-first, GDPR, NIS2, transparency |
| **Reputation Damage** | High | Strict neutrality, fact-checking, error correction |
| **Technology Disruption** | Medium | Modular architecture, cloud-agnostic, adaptable |

## Ethical Boundaries

### What We Will NOT Do
❌ **Partisan Engagement**: Never endorse parties, candidates, political positions
❌ **Paid Content**: No pay-to-play, editorial independence maintained
❌ **Data Manipulation**: No cherry-picking, misrepresentation, deceptive visualizations
❌ **User Exploitation**: No dark patterns, aggressive monetization, privacy violations
❌ **Vendor Lock-In**: Always offer data exports, API access, open standards

### What We WILL Do
✅ **Radical Transparency**: Open source, public ISMS, visible financials
✅ **Stakeholder Diversity**: Serve all political viewpoints equally
✅ **Public Benefit**: Free access for citizens, always
✅ **Ethical Partnerships**: Vet partners for mission alignment
✅ **Sustainable Growth**: Long-term thinking, not growth-at-all-costs

## Remember

- 🌱 **Sustainability First**: Build for long-term viability, not quick wins
- 🤝 **Partnership-Driven**: Leverage complementary organizations
- 💰 **Diversified Revenue**: Multiple streams reduce risk
- 🌍 **Mission Alignment**: All business activities serve transparency goal
- 📊 **Data-Driven Decisions**: Track metrics, optimize, iterate
- 🔒 **Ethical Boundaries**: Never compromise independence, accuracy, neutrality
