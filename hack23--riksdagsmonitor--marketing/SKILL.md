# Marketing Skill


## 🔴 AI FIRST Quality Principle

> **Apply the AI FIRST principle: never accept first-pass quality. Minimum 2 iterations. Read all output, improve every section. No shortcuts.**

## Purpose

Strategic marketing guidance for promoting Riksdagsmonitor's political transparency platform, increasing visibility, engaging stakeholders, and driving adoption through digital channels.

## Core Principles

### 1. 🎯 Mission-Driven Marketing
- **Transparency First**: All marketing must reinforce democratic transparency mission
- **Educational Focus**: Position as knowledge resource, not political actor
- **Trust Building**: Credibility through accuracy, neutrality, and open-source values
- **Public Good**: Emphasize non-commercial, public-benefit nature

### 2. 📊 Data-Driven Approach
- **Analytics-Based**: Use Google Analytics, Search Console for decision-making
- **A/B Testing**: Optimize messaging, CTAs, landing pages
- **KPI Tracking**: Monitor engagement, retention, conversion metrics
- **Attribution**: Understand channel effectiveness

### 3. 🌍 Multi-Channel Strategy
- **Organic Search**: Primary acquisition channel (SEO-first)
- **Social Media**: Engagement on Twitter/X, LinkedIn, Mastodon
- **Content Marketing**: Blog posts, reports, data visualizations
- **Email**: Newsletter for updates, insights (if implemented)
- **Media Relations**: Journalist outreach, press releases

## Compliance & Ethics

### GDPR Compliance
- **No User Tracking Without Consent**: Cookie consent for analytics
- **Privacy-First**: No user accounts, minimal data collection
- **Public Data Only**: All data from official Swedish Parliament sources

### Ethical Marketing
- ❌ **Never**: Make claims about election outcomes, endorse parties
- ✅ **Always**: Cite sources, acknowledge limitations, correct errors promptly
- ✅ **Transparency**: Disclose Hack23 AB ownership, open-source nature
