## The 1.3-Second Hook Rule

### Why 1.3 Seconds?

In 2025, attention spans have shortened to the point where you have just **1.3 seconds** to stop the scroll and capture attention.

### Hook Success Cascade

```
First 1.3 seconds: Scroll stop moment
     |
     v
First 3 seconds: 65% will watch 10+ seconds
     |
     v
First 3 seconds: 45% will watch 30+ seconds
     |
     v
Complete video: Algorithmic boost
     |
     v
Replay: Maximum algorithmic favor
```

### Hook Framework

**Second 1: Visual/Audio Grab**
- Movement in frame
- Unexpected visual
- Pattern interrupt
- Strong audio cue

**Second 2: Value Proposition**
- What will viewer learn/feel?
- Clear benefit statement
- Curiosity amplification

**Second 3: Curiosity Gap**
- Open loop that demands closure
- Question that needs answering
- Promise of payoff

### High-Performing Hook Types

| Hook Type | Example | Best For |
|-----------|---------|----------|
| Curiosity | "Here's what no one tells you about..." | Educational |
| Controversy | "[Popular belief] is completely wrong" | Thought leadership |
| Story | "I was about to give up when..." | Personal brand |
| Result | [Show end result first] | Tutorials |
| FOMO | "Before this gets removed..." | Time-sensitive |
| Self-ID | "POV: You're someone who..." | Niche targeting |

## Looping and Replay Optimization

### Why Loops Matter

TikTok engagement weights:
| Action | Weight |
|--------|--------|
| Likes | 1 |
| Comments | 2 |
| Shares | 3 |
| Complete plays | 4 |
| **Replays** | **5** |

### Creating Seamless Loops

**Technical Requirements:**
- Last frame transitions to first frame
- Audio loops without break
- Story ends where it began
- No jarring cuts at endpoints

**Content Strategies:**
- Reveal then tease in same video
- Circular narratives
- Satisfying processes (art, cooking, ASMR)
- Unexpected endings that require rewatch

### Loop Performance Stats

- 60%+ completion rate = good
- 80%+ completion rate = viral distribution
- Short 10-second loops pull 3x performance of long-form
- 5-10 second loopable clips perform well due to high replay
