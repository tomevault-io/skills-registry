## Scope

- `packaging/**`
- packaging placeholders and release-shape documentation
- packaging-related manifests and matrix rows

## Trigger

- when a prompt is about artifact class, packaging posture, or release-shape placeholders
- when artifact naming must stay consistent with repository naming law

## Do Not Use

- Do not use this skill when the task is host-adapter implementation.
- Do not use this skill when the change is purely governance or inventory with no packaging dimension.
