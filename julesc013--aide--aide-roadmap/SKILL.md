## Scope

- `ROADMAP.md`
- roadmap-oriented phase summaries in root docs
- milestone framing tied to existing plans and implementation evidence

## Trigger

- when a prompt asks for roadmap updates, milestone ordering, or next-phase guidance
- when completed phases need to be reflected in roadmap language
- when committed work must be separated from candidate or future research work

## Do Not Use

- Do not use this skill for backlog promotion decisions that belong in inventory or research first.
- Do not use this skill to add calendar promises or release dates.
