# Lenny Skills: Career & Personal

5 actionable skills for career development.

## Skills

### 1. Startup Ideation
- Start with problems you've experienced
- Validate before building
- Look for underserved markets

### 2. Product-Led Sales Strategy
- Let the product do the selling
- Optimize the self-serve funnel
- Add sales for expansion

### 3. Managing Imposter Syndrome
- Recognize it's common
- Focus on learning, not proving
- Build a support network

### 4. Personal Productivity
- Protect deep work time
- Use systems, not willpower
- Review and iterate weekly

### 5. Organizational Transformation
- Start with why
- Build coalition of supporters
- Celebrate early wins
