# Lenny Skills: Hiring & Team Building

13 actionable skills for building and managing product teams.

## Skills

### 1. Writing Job Descriptions
- Focus on outcomes, not just responsibilities
- Include growth opportunities and team culture
- Be specific about the problems they'll solve

### 2. Conducting Interviews
- Use structured interviews for consistency
- Focus on past behavior, not hypotheticals
- Include work samples or case studies

### 3. Evaluating Candidates
- Define clear rubrics before interviewing
- Look for learning velocity over current skills
- Check for culture add, not just culture fit

### 4. Onboarding New Hires
- Create 30-60-90 day plans
- Assign an onboarding buddy
- Set clear early wins

### 5. Building Team Culture
- Define team values explicitly
- Create rituals that reinforce culture
- Celebrate wins publicly

### 6. Running Effective 1:1s
- Let the report set the agenda
- Focus on coaching, not status updates
- Ask "What's blocking you?"

### 7. Having Difficult Conversations
- Be direct but kind
- Focus on behavior, not personality
- Follow up in writing

### 8. Delegating Work
- Delegate outcomes, not tasks
- Provide context, not just instructions
- Trust but verify

### 9. Managing Up
- Understand your manager's priorities
- Bring solutions, not just problems
- Over-communicate proactively

### 10. Building a Promotion Case
- Document impact continuously
- Seek feedback early and often
- Build visibility across the org

### 11. Negotiating Offers
- Know your market value
- Negotiate the whole package
- Get it in writing

### 12. Finding Mentors & Sponsors
- Mentors advise, sponsors advocate
- Be specific about what you need
- Give before you ask

### 13. Career Transitions
- Build skills before you need them
- Network continuously
- Take calculated risks
