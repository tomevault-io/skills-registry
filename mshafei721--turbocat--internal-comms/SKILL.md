# Internal Communications

## When to Use

Write internal communications for:
- 3P updates (Progress, Plans, Problems)
- Company newsletters
- FAQ responses
- Status reports
- Leadership updates
- Project updates
- Incident reports

## How to Use

1. **Identify the communication type** from the request
2. **Load the appropriate guideline** based on type
3. **Follow specific instructions** for formatting, tone, and content

## Communication Types

### 3P Updates (Progress/Plans/Problems)
Format for team updates covering:
- **Progress**: What was accomplished
- **Plans**: What's coming next
- **Problems**: Blockers and risks

### Company Newsletter
Company-wide updates with:
- Executive summary
- Key wins and milestones
- Team spotlights
- Upcoming events

### FAQ Answers
Structured Q&A format:
- Clear, direct answers
- Supporting context
- Links to resources

### Status Reports
Regular progress reports:
- Metrics and KPIs
- Milestone tracking
- Risk assessment

### Incident Reports
Post-incident documentation:
- Timeline of events
- Impact assessment
- Root cause analysis
- Action items
