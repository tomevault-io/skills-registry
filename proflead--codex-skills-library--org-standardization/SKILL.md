# Org Standardization

## Purpose
Define engineering standards across teams.

## Inputs to request
- Current standards and exceptions.
- Org size and team topology.
- Pain points and compliance needs.

## Workflow
1. Collect current standards and divergences.
2. Propose a minimal common baseline.
3. Define enforcement and adoption plan.

## Output
- Standardization proposal and rollout plan.

## Quality bar
- Balance consistency with team autonomy.
- Define ownership and review cadence.
