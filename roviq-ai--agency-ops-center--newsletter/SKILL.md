# Roviq Newsletter: Beehiiv Content Strategy

This skill governs the creation and management of Roviq newsletter content for Beehiiv.

## 1. Newsletter Structure

The Roviq newsletter has a structured format:

### Header
- **Logo**: Roviq branding.
- **Title**: Issue Number and Date.

### Introduction
- **Hook**: A short, provocative statement about the industry or topic.
- **Summary**: What's in this issue.

### Section 1: Market Intel (The "News")
- **Focus**: High-level trends, competitor moves, or regulatory shifts.
- **Source**: Recent research or industry reports.
- **Takeaway**: Why this matters to operators (clients).

### Section 2: Operator Tips (The "Play")
- **Focus**: Actionable advice for cannabis delivery/retail operators.
- **Example**: "How to optimize your menu for higher conversion" or "Compliance check: age verification at the door".
- **Format**: Bullet points, checklists, or step-by-step guides.

### Section 3: Roviq Updates (The "Inside")
- **Focus**: What we're building, new features, or team wins.
- **Tone**: Humblebrag. "We just shipped X because Y needed it."

### Footer
- **CTA**: "Reply if you want help with [topic]" or link to sales page.
- **Socials**: Twitter/LinkedIn links.
- **Unsubscribe**: Mandatory link.

## 2. Beehiiv Formatting Guidelines

- **Headings**: H2 for main sections, H3 for sub-points. Avoid H1 inside the body.
- **Paragraphs**: Short (2-3 sentences max). Wall of text = delete.
- **Links**: Descriptive anchor text (e.g., "Read the full report here" -> "Read the full report"). No raw URLs.
- **Images**:
  - Max width: 600px.
  - Alt text mandatory.
  - Captions centered below image.
- **Buttons**:
  - Primary CTA button color: `#000000` (Black).
  - Text color: `#FFFFFF` (White).
  - Rounded corners: 4px.

## 3. Tone Guide

**Direct, Data-Driven, No Fluff.** Matches `SOUL.md`.

- **Be specific**: "Revenue increased by 15%" > "Revenue went up significantly".
- **No jargon**: Explain acronyms (e.g., CAC, LTV) if not standard.
- **Opinionated**: Take a stance. "This is bad because..." > "Some say this is bad".
- **Zero "Salesy" Language**: No "Unlock your potential", "Revolutionize your business", or "Game-changer". Just the facts.
- **Respect the Reader**: Assume they are busy operators. Get to the point.

## 4. Content Sections

### Market Intel
- Use `skills/research/SKILL.md` to find relevant news.
- Filter for **impact**: Does this change how they operate tomorrow?

### Operator Tips
- Source from `memory/glass-learnings.md` or client work.
- Make it **immediately useful**.

### Roviq Updates
- Keep it brief. 1-2 sentences.
