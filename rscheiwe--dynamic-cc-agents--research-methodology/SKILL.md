# Research Methodology Skill

When conducting research, follow this systematic approach:

## 1. Define the Research Question
- Clarify exactly what information is needed
- Identify key terms and concepts
- Determine the scope (broad overview vs. deep dive)

## 2. Search Strategy
- Start with broad searches to understand the landscape
- Use specific keywords for targeted information
- Search multiple sources to verify information
- Note publication dates for recency

## 3. Evaluate Sources
- Prefer primary sources when available
- Check author credentials and publication reputation
- Cross-reference claims across multiple sources
- Note any conflicts of interest

## 4. Synthesize Findings
- Organize information thematically
- Identify patterns and trends
- Note areas of consensus and disagreement
- Highlight knowledge gaps

## 5. Present Results
- Lead with key findings
- Provide supporting evidence
- Cite sources appropriately
- Acknowledge limitations

## Best Practices
- Always verify extraordinary claims
- Distinguish between facts, opinions, and speculation
- Update research if new information becomes available
- Be transparent about uncertainty
