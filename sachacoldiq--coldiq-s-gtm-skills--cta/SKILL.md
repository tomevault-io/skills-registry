## CTA Types by Goal

| Goal | CTA Type | Example |
|------|----------|---------|
| **Maximize reach** | Save prompt | "Save this for your next [situation]." |
| **Boost engagement** | Comment prompt | "What's your #1 challenge with [topic]? Drop it below." |
| **Grow followers** | Follow prompt | "I share [topic] frameworks weekly. Follow for more." |
| **Generate leads** | DM prompt | "DM me '[keyword]' and I'll send you the template." |
| **Build email list** | Link-in-comments | "I wrote a full guide on this. Link in comments." |

## CTA Design Rules

1. **One CTA per post** -- multiple CTAs dilute action
2. **Be specific** -- "Comment your biggest hiring mistake" beats "Let me know what you think"
3. **Lower the barrier** -- "Drop a [emoji]" is easier than "Write a paragraph"
4. **Match CTA to content** -- frameworks = save, opinions = comment, personal brand = follow
5. **Saves are weighted 5x** -- always consider "is this saveable?" when writing

## High-Performing CTA Templates

**Save-driving:** "Bookmark this for next time you [situation]. You'll thank yourself."

**Comment-driving:** "I'm curious -- what would you add to this list? Drop your #1 in the comments."

**Debate-driving:** "Agree or disagree? I'll respond to every comment."

**Follow-driving:** "I break down [niche] strategies like this every week. Follow along."

**DM-driving:** "Want the full [template/checklist/framework]? DM me '[keyword]' and I'll send it over."

## Profile as Conversion Funnel

Your profile is a landing page. Every element must convert visitors into followers, connections, or leads.

### Headline Formulas (220 characters max)

| Formula | Example |
|---------|---------|
| "I help [audience] [result]" | "I help B2B SaaS companies generate pipeline through cold outreach" |
| "Role \| Niche + Proof" | "Head of Growth @ Acme \| Helped 3 startups reach $10M ARR" |
| "Role \| What you do \| Social proof" | "Fractional CMO \| Revenue marketing for B2B \| Former HubSpot" |

**Avoid:** "visionary," "guru," "ninja," "thought leader," or job title alone.

### About Section (2,600 chars max)

```
[HOOK - First 2 lines visible before "see more"]
[PROBLEM YOU SOLVE - 2-3 sentences]
[YOUR APPROACH - 3-5 sentences]
[PROOF - 3-5 bullet points with specific results]
[WHO I WORK WITH - 2-3 sentences]
[CTA - exact next step: DM, email, book a call]
```

### Featured Section (Priority Order)

1. Lead magnet / free resource (drives email list)
2. Best-performing LinkedIn post (social proof)
3. Case study / testimonial (proof of results)
4. Newsletter (subscriber growth)
5. Media appearance (authority)

Max 3-5 items. Most important = leftmost. Update monthly.

### Banner Design

- Dimensions: **1584 x 396 pixels** (4:1 ratio)
- Keep critical text in center (edges cropped on mobile)
- Include: tagline, website URL, key offering, or social proof

## Comment Gating (Highest Engagement CTA)

Comment-gated posts drive 3-5x normal comment volume. The algorithm sees massive comments → boosts distribution.

**Keyword Comment Gate:**
```
Comment "INTENT" below and I'll share it.
Comment "STACK" and I'll send you the full breakdown.
Comment "CLAUDE" and make sure we're connected so I can DM it to you.
```

**P.S. Question Closers (proven highest-performing):**
```
P.S: What tool should I add to this stack?
P.S: How are your cold email campaigns doing right now?
P.S: What's the ONE tool you can't live without?
P.S: Which part of this system would you want more detail on?
```

**Continuation Tease:**
```
I'm running out of space... Continuing in the comments 👇
Make sure to follow so you don't miss the next breakdown.
```

**Example 1** -- Post about a sales framework (goal: saves):
End with: "Save this framework for your next pipeline review. Repost if your team needs it too."
