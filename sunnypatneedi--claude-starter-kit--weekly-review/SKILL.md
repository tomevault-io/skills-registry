# Weekly Review

Guide me through a comprehensive weekly review:

## Part 1: Reflect on the Past Week

1. **Accomplishments**: What did I complete this week?
2. **Progress**: How did I advance on my main goals?
3. **Wins**: What unexpected good things happened?
4. **Challenges**: What obstacles did I face?
5. **Lessons**: What did I learn?

## Part 2: Clear the Mind

1. **Open loops**: What's lingering that needs attention?
2. **Inbox zero**: Any messages/tasks needing processing?
3. **Commitments**: Did I keep all my promises?

## Part 3: Plan Next Week

1. **Big Rocks**: What are my top 3-5 priorities?
2. **Calendar**: What's already scheduled?
3. **Projects**: What needs to move forward?
4. **Habits**: Which habits am I focusing on?

## Part 4: Wellbeing Check

1. **Energy**: How was my energy this week?
2. **Balance**: Work/life balance rating?
3. **Relationships**: Who do I need to connect with?
4. **Self-care**: What do I need more/less of?

Ask probing questions and help me think deeper about each area.
