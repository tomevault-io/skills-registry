# LinkedIn Content Writer

Creates engaging LinkedIn post content focused on business updates and sales generation, using company information from documents like the Hackathon Document.

## When to Use This Skill

Use this skill when you need to:
- Generate LinkedIn posts about business updates
- Create content that generates sales leads
- Share company achievements and industry insights
- Highlight business value to potential customers
- Draft professional LinkedIn content based on company information

## Information Sources

This skill draws from company documentation like the Hackathon Document to include relevant business context:

- Personal AI Employee project focus
- Digital FTE (Full-Time Equivalent) concepts
- Automation and productivity benefits
- Cost efficiency and scalability advantages
- Technology stack (Claude Code, Obsidian, MCP servers)

## Content Guidelines

When creating LinkedIn posts, follow these principles:

### Professional Tone
- Maintain a professional, business-focused tone
- Use industry-appropriate language
- Avoid overly casual language

### Value Proposition
- Highlight business value and benefits
- Focus on problem-solving capabilities
- Emphasize efficiency and productivity gains

### Audience Awareness
- Target potential customers and business partners
- Address business pain points
- Showcase relevant expertise

### Structure
- Start with an engaging hook
- Provide valuable insights or information
- End with a clear call to action or thought-provoking question

## Content Themes

Draw from these themes based on available company information:

- AI automation and digital transformation
- Productivity enhancement through technology
- Cost reduction and efficiency gains
- Business scalability solutions
- Industry innovation and trends

## Output Format

Generate content that includes:
1. An attention-grabbing opening
2. Valuable business insights or information
3. Connection to company expertise
4. Professional closing that encourages engagement
