# Monetization Portfolio Design

## Intent
- Balance user delight with sustainable revenue across devices, regions, and regulatory regimes.
- Prototype monetization mechanics early to validate willingness-to-pay and economic viability.

## Inputs
1. Current revenue streams + unit economics (ARPU, payer mix, whale dependency, gas costs).
2. Competitive benchmarks (pricing ladders, battle passes, loot tables, NFT drops, premium tiers).
3. Compliance requirements (app store rules, KYC/AML, regional taxes).

## Workflow
1. **Value exchange mapping**
   - Identify core value units (time, status, access, ownership) and user motivations.
2. **Portfolio ideation**
   - Generate monetization concepts across low-friction (ads, referrals) to high-commitment (subscriptions, ownership, DeFi yield).
   - Define guardrails (pay-to-win avoidance, fairness, secondary market policy).
3. **Economic modeling**
   - Build lightweight spreadsheets or tokenomics sims for each concept; stress-test retention impact and margin.
4. **Validation path**
   - Outline pricing experiments (offer tests, bundles, limited-time events) and required instrumentation.
   - Align cross-functional sign-offs (legal, finance, platform, community).

## Verification
- Every monetization concept includes user value narrative + downside risks.
- Economic models reviewed with finance/analytics; assumptions documented.
- Experiments logged with success metrics (conversion, payer NPS, churn impact).
