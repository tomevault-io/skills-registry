# Founder Questions Generator

Create interview questions that extract authentic, emotional content from founders. Stories are 22x more memorable than facts alone.

## Why Founder Ads Work

- **Uncopyable**: Competitors cannot replicate your founder
- **AI-proof**: AI cannot fake authentic founder presence (yet)
- **Long-lasting**: Founder ads run for years without burnout (vs 2 weeks for UGC)
- **High trust**: We evolved to trust faces before brands, before we could read
- **Social proof without telling**: Shows the founder putting their name on the line

### Real Results (Kamal FounderAds)
- $380K spent in ONE DAY on single founder ad
- 300K to 1.5M in 2 months with one founder ad
- UK brand: 2M/month at 14x ROAS, top spender is founder ad
- 1K/day to 15K/day in 48 hours, CPA dropped from $42 to $15

## Process

### Step 1: Understand Product/Brand

Gather: product/service, industry, target customer, origin story, unique features, common objections.

### Step 2: Generate Questions by Category

**ORIGIN STORY (10-15 questions)**
- Why did you start this company?
- What was the moment you knew you had to create this?
- What did people say when you told them your idea?
- What was your lowest point building this?
- When did you know this was actually going to work?
- What problem were you trying to solve for yourself first?
- What were you doing before this, and why did you leave?
- Who told you this would never work?
- What almost made you quit?
- What kept you going when things got hard?

**CUSTOMER SURPRISES (10-15 questions)**
- What's the most surprising thing a customer ever told you?
  - (This question alone has made Kamal "millions and millions")
- What's the craziest compliment you've ever received?
- What's the wildest compliment you've ever received?
- Has a customer ever made you cry? What happened?
- What customer story do you tell at dinner parties?
- What result has a customer gotten that even surprised you?
- What customer message do you keep coming back to?
- What's a customer win that still gives you chills?
- Tell me about a customer who almost didn't buy but became a superfan
- What customer transformation shocked even you?

**INDUSTRY FRUSTRATIONS (10-15 questions)**
- What annoys you most about your industry?
- What do other companies get wrong?
- What's the biggest lie told in your space?
- What did you refuse to do that everyone else does?
- What's the dirty secret of your industry?
- What makes you angry about how others treat customers?
- What's your brand NOT?
- What's the industry standard you broke?
- Why do most products in your category fail?
- What do customers deserve that they're not getting?

**PRODUCT DEEP-DIVE (10-15 questions)**
- What makes this actually work?
- What ingredient/feature are you most proud of?
- How many iterations did you go through?
- What do people not realize about the product?
- What's the one thing you obsessed over that nobody notices?
- Why does this cost what it costs?
- What did you put in that nobody else does?
- What takes the longest to get right?
- What would you never compromise on?
- How is this made differently than competitors?

**OBJECTION HANDLING (10-15 questions)**
- What do skeptics say, and how do you respond?
- Why should someone trust you?
- What if someone says it's too expensive?
- What would you say to someone on the fence?
- What's the most common reason people don't buy?
- What would you tell yourself before you tried this?
- Why does this work when other things don't?
- What if someone has been burned before?
- How do you respond to your 1-star reviews?
- What guarantee can you make?

**EMOTIONAL TRIGGERS (10-15 questions)**
- What does it feel like when you read a positive review?
- When did you first realize you were actually helping people?
- What moment are you most proud of?
- What story still gives you chills?
- What makes you cry (happy or sad) about this business?
- When did you know you had something real?
- What customer message do you screenshot and save?
- What would your mom/dad say about what you've built?
- When did you feel like you made it?
- What's the legacy you want to leave?

**LIFE FORCE 8 HOOKS (designed to hit core desires)**
- What do customers say about their confidence after using this?
- How does this help people save time with their family?
- What do customers' partners/spouses say?
- How has this helped someone's career or income?
- What do people say about feeling safe/secure?
- How does this make people feel more attractive?
- What comfort does this bring to their daily life?
- How does this help people be better parents/partners?

### Step 3: Add Follow-Up Prompts

Use these to dig deeper after any answer:
- "Tell me more about that..."
- "What happened next?"
- "How did that make you feel?"
- "Can you give me a specific example?"
- "Take me back to that moment..."
- "What were you thinking when that happened?"
- "What did they actually say, in their words?"

### Step 4: The Stories > Statements Principle

Stories are 22x more memorable than facts alone. Train founders to speak in stories, not claims.

| Instead of (Statement) | Get them to say (Story) |
|------------------------|-------------------------|
| "The clothes are comfortable" | "I can get on the floor with my kids in this" |
| "Customers love it" | "One customer told me they wear my pants to get a job or get laid" |
| "It's high quality" | "I read every review and one customer's mom wore our necklace every day until she passed, now her daughter wears it" |
| "It works" | "Christie messaged me and said her mom wore her initial necklace every single day..." |

**Question to elicit stories:**
- "Tell me about a specific customer, what was their name, what happened?"
- "Walk me through exactly what happened that day..."
- "What did they actually say to you?"

### Step 5: Organize by Funnel Stage

**TOP (Awareness)**: Origin story, industry frustrations, emotional hooks
**MIDDLE (Consideration)**: Product deep-dive, customer surprises, how it works
**BOTTOM (Decision)**: Objection handling, trust questions, guarantees, risk reversal

## Interview Execution Tips (Kamal FounderAds)

### The Golden Rules
1. **NEVER give them a script** - They'll talk like their website
2. **NEVER share questions in advance** - Authentic reactions only
3. **Ask questions they haven't prepared for** - Catch them off guard
4. **Wait through awkward silences** - Gold comes AFTER the pause
5. **Stories > Statements** - 22x more memorable
6. **Capture the pain in their eyes** - Authenticity is visible

### What Goes Wrong
- Giving founders scripts (they freeze up)
- Expensive studios (makes them stiff)
- Formal clothing (uncomfortable = unnatural)
- Sharing questions beforehand (rehearsed = fake)
- Expecting every answer to be good (you need volume)

### What Works
- Casual conversation format
- Interesting backgrounds, not sterile
- 100 questions in 1-2 hours
- Following up with "tell me more"
- Letting them ramble (edit later)
- Mining for that ONE golden moment

## Post-Interview Processing

### Traditional Method
After shoot, organize Google Drive folders:
- Clips labeled with timestamps
- Organized by question category
- Makes editing 10x faster

### AI-Assisted Method (Claude)
1. Get full 1-2 hour shoot transcribed with timestamps
2. Feed transcript to Claude project trained on your ad system
3. Prompt: "Using ONLY exact language from this transcript, create a script my editor can cut"
4. Claude identifies best clips and assembles them

## Volume and Testing

From 1-2 hour shoot you get:
- ~100 clips total
- ~20 potential hooks
- ~30 social proof moments
- ~10 narrative segments

These can be remixed into dozens of ad variations:
- Hook + Body + CTA combinations
- Different hooks, same body
- Same hook, different angles
- Funnel-specific versions

## Why Founder Ads Don't Burn Out

UGC burns out in ~2 weeks. Founder ads run for YEARS because:
- Authenticity doesn't expire
- Trust compounds over time
- Story stays relevant
- No "I've seen this creator before" fatigue
- Creates parasocial relationship with brand

## When Founder Doesn't Match ICP

Options if founder doesn't look like the customer:
- Head of Product can substitute
- Customer service lead (very authentic)
- Founder's girlfriend/partner (if matches demo)
- Focus on voice-over with B-roll if needed

Note: Kamal has seen male founders selling to young women with top-spending founder ads. Authenticity can overcome demographic mismatch.
