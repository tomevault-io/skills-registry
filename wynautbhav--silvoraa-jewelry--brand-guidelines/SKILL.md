# Brand Guidelines Skill

## Visual Identity
- **Primary Colors**: Adhere strictly to the project's defined palette (e.g., in `tailwind.config.js`).
- **Typography**: Use the designated font family. Do not mix more than 2 fonts.
- **Logo Usage**: Ensure sufficient clear space around logos.

## Voice & Tone
- **Professional but Approachable**: informative, clear, and concise.
- **User-Centric**: Speak to the user ("You can update your profile") rather than system-centric ("Profile update successful").
